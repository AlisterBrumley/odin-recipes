Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

Description

Peanut butter on bread, 'Jelly' or Jam; also on bread.
Peanut butter bread, on top of Jelly bread.
Best Sandwich.


Jelly and Jam arent the same thing, but you cant always find one or the other, so get what you can

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly or Jam

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on bread
  2. Spread Jelly/Jam on bread
  3. Slap the two together
  4. Make two, why not?
