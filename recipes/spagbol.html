Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

What needs to be said? Spaghetti pasta and Bolognese sauce. Its probably not accurate to what italians actually do, but it tastes good, so fuhgeddaboudit!

Ingredients

  • 500g of spaghetti
  • 500g of mince or whatever subsitute for vegan/dietary conditions
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Whatever vegetables you want
  • Too much olive oil
  • More tomato paste

Steps

  1. Prepare stuff
  2. Heat up oil on medium heat
  3. Add onions, garlic and cook for 2 minutes or so
  4. Add meat and cook until brown
  5. Add tomato paste
  6. No, more tomato paste
  7. Add the rest of the vegetables and simmer
  8. In another pan, boil water for pasta
  9. Cook said pasta
  10. Drain pasta and serve
